Ebenwalk is the concatenation of my name. Eben Walk, little known to me at the time of creating my profile and website, is actually a sort of seasonal, natural event whereby the Eben Ice Caves are formed in the Hiawatha National Forest in Michigan. But enough about that.

I started learning code a while ago. I was working in a quiet museum digitizing their collection. The software didn’t do exactly what I wanted it to do and my work was seen by only a select few colleagues within the museum. I wanted to fix that and I wanted the museum to be able to share all my work and their collection with the greater public. At the time, I didn't know it would become a passion of mine.

I began to tinker, read all the books, taking all the courses, attending every webinar, and listening to every podcast I could. I would have never imagined myself here, yet, here I am. Building things, creating applications, developing programs! Coding has become my passion and my mission.

To check out what I’ve built, head on over to my projects page. To learn more about my story and things I've learned check out my blog. If your interested in working with me you can find me on linkedin or via my contact page.
